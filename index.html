About the product

🏡 Nurtured in the Village Tradition 🏡 In a world of modern shortcuts, we proudly embrace the timeless techniques passed down through generations. Our ghee is slow-cooked in small batches over an open flame, allowing it to develop its distinct nutty flavor and exquisite texture. This methodical process harkens back to the days when communities gathered around the hearth, creating a ghee that's not just food but a sentiment.
